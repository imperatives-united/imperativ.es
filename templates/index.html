Imperatives

Mission

Take part in real, lasting impact and an unambiguously better world by focusing on imperative issues.

Structure

Imperatives is a united coallition of special interest groups who are sick of political red tape and only want to make the world a better place for all. While our constituents may only be invested in some of the imperatives we champion, we find strength in our collective strength to mobilize, help each other where possible, and enact lasting change. We operate primarily through Slack, with each imperative having its own channel, and occassionally structure in-person meetings and online calls to share ideas, share connections, and figure out next steps.

How are imperatives choosen?

Imperatives was started by a group of people who shared the fundamental belief that, we can't do anything lasting if the human race becomes extinct or our planet is destroyed. This seemed natural as the first imperative on which to focus. As we gain momentum the community will decide how new imperatives should be added

Imperatives

  • Global Catastrophe & Existential Risk - The ongoing survival of the human race and the planet we live on.
